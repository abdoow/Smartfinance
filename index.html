Salary Calculator

Not sure what tax rate to use? Here's a general guide:
• Lower income (~$10,000 - $44,725): ~12%
• Middle income ($44,726 - $95,375): ~22%
• Higher income ($95,376 - $182,100): ~24%
Please note, actual rates may vary. Check official sources for exact rates.

Car Payment Calculator

Need help figuring out your car payment? Here's what each field means:
• Car Price: Total price of the car you're buying.
• Down Payment: Amount you pay upfront (typically 10-20% of the car price).
• Loan Term: Usually between 36 and 72 months. Shorter terms have higher monthly payments but less interest.
• Interest Rate: Average rates range between 3-7%, depending on your credit score.
Check current market rates or your lender for exact details.

Mortgage Calculator

Confused about mortgage details? Here's a simple explanation:
• Home Price: The total price of the home you're purchasing.
• Down Payment: Usually about 10-20% of the home's price, paid upfront.
• Loan Term: Common terms are 15 or 30 years. A shorter loan term means higher monthly payments but lower overall interest.
• Interest Rate: Typical mortgage rates range from 3% to 8%, depending on your credit and market conditions.
Always verify with your lender for the most accurate numbers.

Budgeting Tool

Not sure how to use the budgeting tool? Here's a quick guide:
• Monthly Income: Your total monthly earnings after taxes.
• Rent/Bills: Total monthly payments for housing, utilities, and other fixed expenses.
• Savings Goal (%): How much of your leftover money you want to save, typically 10-20%.
This tool helps you see clearly how much money you can spend each month.
